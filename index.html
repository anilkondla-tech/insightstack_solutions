03 · How we work

A process
engineered for trust.

Every engagement follows the same four phases. No vague proposals, no surprise invoices — every milestone has a deliverable you can demo to your team.

01

Discover

A 2-week immersion. We sit with your team, audit current pipelines, identify the metric that matters, and write a one-page brief everyone signs off on.

02

Architect

We design the smallest viable system that can grow. Pipelines, schemas, dashboards and access — all reviewed before a line of production code is written.

03

Build & ship

Weekly demos. Code reviewed in your repo, tests gated in your CI, dashboards landed in your BI tool. You see progress every Friday — never quarterly.

04

Hand off & coach

We don't want to live in your stack forever. Documentation, runbooks and a 90-day coaching tail mean your team owns it when we step back.
